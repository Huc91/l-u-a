What does it mean to be human in a World where more and more intelligent computers are taking over tasks that for centuries have belonged to human beings, and have been identified with the concept of "human being"?

The cultural association Umanesimo Artificiale is made up of young creative people and professionals from the Marche region, with national and international contaminations.

The intent of the association is to question the meaning and the role of the human being in the digital age, dominated by artificial intelligences.

Art is the channel through which we try to reach the most heterogeneous audience possible, and the instrument to celebrate in its essence the interaction between man and machine.
